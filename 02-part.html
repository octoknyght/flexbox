02. flex-1 (flex: 1 1 0%)

a. add a class of flex-1 to both #cover and #details

- this allows flex items (#cover and #details) to grow and shrink as needed, ignoring their initial size

- since there are 2 flex items, they will now share the parent's height and width (#banner) giving them 50% each

b. add a background color of blue-100 to cover and red-100 to details

- TIP 1: using lighter colors are better when experimenting since they're easier on the eyes

- TIP 2: using background colors are better than borders since border sizes affects the element's size
