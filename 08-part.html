08. Margins

a. delete all the labels (user info, left, right, 1, 2, 3, 4 ...), and remove the text-center class from the body tag

- they affect the total height of the containing div

b. add m-4 on both #left and #right, and ml-4 on the #avatar

- hit refresh and resize window to see the changes

c. add space-y-4 on #right, and on its 4 children, a class of h-4

- to evenly space social items, and give them height

d. add p-20 to #cover

- to give them a temporary height even if there's no image/content

e. add a div between #left and #right, give it an id of #divider and a class of "my-4 border-b-2 border-cyan-300 border-dashed"

- this will only show on smaller screens
